Frequently Asked Questions

How do I return an item?
To return an item, log in to your account and visit the Orders page. Select the order, click "Request Return," and choose a reason. We email a prepaid return label within one business day. Pack the item in its original packaging and drop it off at any authorized carrier location. See our return policy for windows and exceptions.

What is your warranty policy?
All physical products carry a 1-year limited manufacturer warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty does not cover accidental damage, normal wear, or unauthorized modifications. To file a warranty claim, contact support with your order number and a photo of the issue.

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, and Shop Pay. We do not accept checks, money orders, cryptocurrency, or store credit from other retailers. All transactions are processed in USD.

How do I create an account?
Click "Sign Up" in the top-right corner of any page. You will need a valid email address and a password of at least 12 characters. Account creation is free, and you can check out as a guest if you prefer not to register.

How do I unsubscribe from the newsletter?
Click the "Unsubscribe" link at the bottom of any marketing email, or visit Account Settings > Communication Preferences and toggle marketing emails off. Order confirmations and shipping updates are transactional and cannot be disabled.
