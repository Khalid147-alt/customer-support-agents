Shipping Information

Standard shipping. Standard shipping takes 5 to 7 business days from the order ship date and is free on all orders over $50. For orders under $50, the standard shipping fee is $5.99. Standard shipping is available to all 50 US states and Puerto Rico.

Express shipping. Express shipping arrives in 2 to 3 business days for a flat rate of $12.99 regardless of order size. Orders placed before 2 PM Eastern Time on a business day ship the same day. Orders placed after 2 PM, on weekends, or on US holidays ship the next business day.

International shipping. We ship to over 40 countries with delivery in 10 to 14 business days. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination. Customs fees, duties, and import taxes are the customer's responsibility and are not included in the shipping fee.

Tracking. A tracking number is sent to your email within 24 hours of dispatch. You can also view tracking on the Orders page of your account at any time. If you have not received tracking after 48 hours, please contact support with your order number.
