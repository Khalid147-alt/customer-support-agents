Return Policy

Return window. You may return most items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund of the item price. The 30-day clock starts the day the carrier marks your shipment as delivered.

Condition requirements. Items must be returned unused, in their original packaging, with all tags, accessories, and manuals included. Items returned in worn or damaged condition may be subject to a restocking fee of up to 20%, or refused entirely.

Refund timeline. Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. You will receive an email confirmation when the refund is processed. Bank posting times vary and may add 1 to 3 additional business days.

Exceptions. Digital products, downloadable software licenses, and gift cards are non-refundable once delivered. Items marked "Final Sale" on the product page cannot be returned. Personalized or custom-engraved items are non-returnable unless defective.

Defective or damaged items. If your item arrives defective or damaged, contact support within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund and cover all return shipping costs.
